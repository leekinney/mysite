Monolith Skate was started by two longtime friends, Irv Velez and Jon Gillman. We decided to start the company because of our shared passion for the sport and culture of skateboarding. We have been skating together since we first met in 1986 and offer expertise based on these deep roots. We strive to provide the products and service that every skateboarder from any generation needs. It is our goal to inspire the next generation of skateboarders while bridging the age and style gap to unify the community.
