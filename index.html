Who I am...

My name is LeeAnn and I am a front-end web developer living in Philadelphia, PA. I like to create websites that are user friendly and implement the latest web standards. I have been learning about developing and designing websites for the past few years and loving every minute of it. Along with my recently completed web design & development certificate, I have also been taking classes through Girl Develop It, Codagogy & Code School.

I have a well-organized work style and the ability to manage multiple projects while consistently meeting deadlines. I am a fast learning, team player with strong analytical and troubleshooting skills. Through my various positions working with diverse backgrounds, I have developed excellent communication and customer service skills along with project management abilities. I am an enthusiastic and highly motivated web professional with a history of working well under pressure.

I have experience with HTML5, CSS3, jQuery, Responsive Design, SEO, Adobe Creative Suite & WordPress. I also have basic knowledge of PHP, MySQL and SASS. Currently, I am working on teaching myself GIT and working to gain further knowledge of JavaScript. When I'm not tinkering with code, taking classes or trying to consume as much knowledge about the web as possible I can be found developing and blogging over here, attempting to restore my 1970 Honda CB350, baking, biking or exploring.
